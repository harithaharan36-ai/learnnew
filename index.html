Who We Are

Born from obsession

"The difference between good coffee and great coffee is the story behind every bean."

DRIP started in a tiny roastery in 2019 with one obsession: find the world's best coffee and get out of its way. No gimmicks. No flavored syrups hiding mediocre beans. Just the purest expression of what coffee can be.

We work directly with 14 farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Japan. We visit every harvest. We know every farmer by name.
